A carbon laser peel is a treatment that helps with acne, uneven skin tone enlarged pores and oily skin. They’re sometimes called carbon laser facials. Despite the name, a carbon laser peel isn’t a traditional chemical peel. Instead, we use carbon solution and lasers to create a peeling effect.

How does laser carbon peel work?

Laser carbon peel is a non-invasive cosmetic procedure that rejuvenates the skin's appearance. It involves applying a thin layer of carbon solution onto the skin, which is then targeted with a specialized laser. When the laser is passed over the skin, it heats and vaporizes the carbon particles, gently exfoliating the outermost layer of dead skin cells. This process stimulates collagen production and promotes skin renewal.

The treatment also targets and reduces the appearance of fine lines, wrinkles, acne scars, and pigmentation irregularities. Laser carbon peel is a quick, safe, and effective way to achieve smoother, more youthful-looking skin.

Answers to frequently asked questions

What can I expect during a laser carbon peel session?

During a laser carbon peel session, a thin layer of carbon solution will be applied to your skin. After allowing it to dry, a specialized laser will be used to heat and vaporize the carbon particles, exfoliating the outer layer of your skin. You may feel a mild warming sensation during the treatment, but it is generally well-tolerated and non-painful. The session typically takes about 30 minutes to an hour, depending on the area being treated.
